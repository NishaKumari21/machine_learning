Email classification is a crucial task in the field of natural language processing. By analyzing the content of an email, we can determine whether it falls into categories such as 'Spam' or 'Ham' (legitimate email). This helps in filtering out unwanted emails and ensures that important messages reach your inbox. The classification is typically performed using machine learning algorithms, which are trained on large datasets of emails to learn the patterns associated with each category.
